About Us

Who We Are

Bila Business Intelligence Institute is South African company established in 2006, currently 100% black owned and managed. Bila Business Intelligence Institute provides customers with effective enterprise delivery framework that rapidly unlock the power critical business data, allowing our customers to timeously identify, analyse and act on strategic information to gain competitive advantage.

Bila Business Intelligence Institute core competencies include:

  • Business Intelligence
  • Performance Management
  • Project Scoping
  • Planning & Implementation
  • Data Quality Management
  • Data Warehousing

Philosophy

Bila Busines Intelligence Institute service philosophy is to work close partnership with customer to ensure long term relationship that delivers sustainable value for the business. Our professional team have a passion for delivering innovative solutions that empower people an organization to make better decisions.

Our consultants operates under our code of ethics, which encompasses integrity,, honesty, and communication that encourages teamwork. This forms the foundation of each and every customer engagement.
